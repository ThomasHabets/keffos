Exceptions on the ia32

Exception: Function:
(hex)	 
--------------------------
0	   Divide error
1	   Debug exception
2	   Nonmaskable interrupt
3	   Breakpoint
4	   overflow
5	   bounds check
6	   invalid opcode
7	   Coprocessor not availible
8	   Double fault
9	   (reserved)
A	   Invalid TSS
B	   Segment not present
D	   Stack exception
C	   General protection
E	   Page fault
F	   (reserved)
10	   Coprocessor error
11-1F	   (reserved)
20-FF	   Availible for external interrupts via INTR pin
